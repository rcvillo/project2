Hawaii

SAIL AROUND THE ISLAND

Claire, Paige, and Raeanne depart the hotel at 7:00am to head over to the dock. They are welcomed by their two "captains" who help them onto a small raft. However, they certainly underestimated the speed of it to the point where they were so sore from grasping onto the ropes on the inside of the raft so that they didn't fall overboard. During the ride, they were given the chance to snorkel where they saw a pod of dolphins among many other sea creatures.
